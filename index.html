What I Do

As System Development Specialist

I have been doing activities related to product development (websites, programs, interfaces, etc). Starting from doing some research, and then defining the problem, analyzing and designing the solution to that problem (even the UI and UX design too, if necessary), collaborating with IT engineering teams during the development phase, SIT and UAT with any related teams, deploying the products, until evaluating the results to ensure the problem has been solved.
